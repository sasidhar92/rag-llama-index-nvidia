Lions are big cats that live in Africa.
They have light blue and pale gold fur and males have manes.
Lions hunt in groups called prides.
Elephants are the largest land animals.
They have long trunks and big ears.
Elephants live in herds and eat plants.
Penguins are birds that can't fly.
They swim very well in cold water.
Penguins live in the Southern Hemisphere.
Dolphins are smart mammals that live in the ocean.
They use echolocation to find food.
Dolphins are known for being friendly to humans.
Giraffes have very long necks.
They eat leaves from tall trees.
Giraffes run fast but can't make loud noises.
